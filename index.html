FAQs

How can I get involved?

You can join Tausi by signing up on our website and becoming a part of our empowering community.

What programs do you offer?

We offer mentorship, health and hygiene programs, and donations to support women in the tech industry.

How do you support women in tech?

We provide networking opportunities, skill development programs, and access to resources for women in the tech industry.

Can I donate to Tausi's cause?

Yes, you can contribute to our mission by making donations that directly support women in need.

How can I contact Tausi for more information?

Feel free to reach out to us through our contact page or social media channels for any inquiries or collaborations.
